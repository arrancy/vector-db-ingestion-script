destination : Andaman & Nicobar Islands

They are known for pristine beaches, excellent scuba‑diving (especially around Havelock and Neil Islands), tropical forests and a mix of cultures. Most tourists stay on the Andaman side; only a few islands are open to visitors.
best time to visit : Mid‑January to mid‑May (dry, sunny, good diving conditions)
ideal duration : 5–7 days
